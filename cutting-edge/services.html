Services

Mowing

Good mowing is essential to every lawn. At Cutting Edge, we provide flexible mowing options that best suit your lawn. We provide options for discharge methods to use on your lawn, or we can decide based on your lawn's current condition. We also provide control over your grass length, so your lawn looks exactly how you like it. At Cutting Edge, we use electric lawn mowers, ensuring quiet performance.

Edging And Weed Whacking

Edging is crucial for maintaining a consistent edge on your grass. As well as offering edging services, we also offer weed control services, so the cracks in your pavement stay free of weeds. Similar to mowing, we also use electric tools for this process, which keeps noice levels at a minimum.

Blowing

Having a leaf-free lawn is important; not only for looks, but ensuring that no dead grass patches form. We use a high power gasoline blower that keeps leaves off your lawn as well as keeping your pavement clear.
